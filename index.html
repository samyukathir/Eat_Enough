    Services

    Flexible Nutrition Targets

    Complex nutrition or macro schemes? Never fear, we're here for you. Set different targets for each day of the week and make quick adjustments if you miss a day.

    Quick Ingredient Filters

    Keyword filters ensure you only see foods that fit your specific diet or meal plan needs. You can also block specific ingredients if you see something you just don't like.

    Easy Recurring Foods

    Whether it's due to diet requirements, limited time for cooking, or just a desire to keep things simple, recurring dishes allow you to put your favorite foods and meals on repeat.

    Personalized Ratings

    Our rating system lets you teach us about what you like so we can improve our recommendations, and what you dislike so we don't offer it again.

    Endless Regeneration

    Sometimes a meal idea doesn't fit exactly what you need or just doesn't sound good in the moment. If that's the case, a new option is always one click away.

    Cost-Saving Pantry

    Our pantry lets you track everything you have on hand for meals so you know when foods (especially the preservative-free ones!) need to be replaced. Easily see meal suggestions that use up what's in your pantry.

    Do You Know?

    Writing down a plan doubles the chance of success! In the book Atomic Habits, author James Clear talks about a study in the British Journal of Health Psychology that showed 91% of people who wrote down their intention on a goal (in their case, exercise) followed through. This was more than double that of people who didn't write it down. Eat This Much helps you set your intentions every single week by generating specific meal plans for you to follow!
